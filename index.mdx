IronClaw is a secure, open-source AI agent framework built in Rust and deployed on NEAR AI Cloud. It enables creating AI agents with access to your tools and services, while keeping your credentials safe and private.

## Why IronClaw?

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Encrypted Vault" icon="lock">
    Credentials are injected into requests only for endpoints you've approved.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sandboxed Tools" icon="box">
    A compromised tool can't reach anything outside its allowlist.
  </Card>
  <Card title="NEAR AI Cloud" icon="shield-halved">
    Your data and prompts stay truly private
  </Card>
  <Card title="Built in Rust" icon="rust">
    Memory safety enforced at compile time.
  </Card>
</Columns>
